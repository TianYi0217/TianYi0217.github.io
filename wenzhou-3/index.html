Nanxi River

4A-level scenic area in Yongjia County, Zhejiang, with stunning landscapes and adjacent to Yandang Mountain and Jinyun Xiandu.

Baizhang Stream

A national 4A-level waterfall in Wencheng County, Wenzhou, known for its natural beauty since its designation as a key scenic spot in 2004.

Nanji Islands

A national nature reserve consisting of Nanji Island, located 30 nautical miles off Pingyang County, representing a unique marine ecosystem.

Dongtou

One of China's 14 island districts, composed of 168 islands, embellishing Wenzhou as the "County of a Hundred Islands" and the "Pearl of the East China Sea."

Nan Yandang Mountain

4A-level scenic area in Pingyang County, offering breathtaking views and located 87 kilometers north of Wenzhou City.

Xiwan

Situated in Pingyang County, the scenic area stands out with its numerous bays, wide beaches, deep caves, and striking reefs.

Xiwan

One of "Four Famous Isolated Islands," representing Wenzhou's uniqueness with its picturesque landscape and architectural landmarks.
