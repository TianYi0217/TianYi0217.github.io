Impression nantang

Impression Nantang" scenic area is located in the historical and cultural essence of Wenzhou, but also was the scar area of a modern civilized city, through a large number of protection and use of engineering construction and social investment, now"Impression Nantang" has become a comprehensive and diversified cultural and leisure tourism area.

Nanxi River

Nanxi River is located in Yongjia County in the northern part of Wenzhou, Zhejiang Province. It is 26km south of Wenzhou City. It is adjacent to Yandangshan Geopark city in the east and Jinyun City in the west. Nanxi River is a national AAAA-class tourist area, a List of national parks of China, and a global geopark.

100 feet long

100 feet long, located in the great waterfall of Wencheng County, Wenzhou, Zhejiang Province. In 2004, the scenic spot was classified as a national 4a-class scenic spot, and the nearby scenic spot was List of national parks of China.

Dongtou

Dongtou is one of the 14 island districts (cities and counties) in China. It is located in the southeast of Oujiang estuary, 68 kilometers away from Wenzhou. The whole district consists of 168 islands, like a string of bright pearls, inlaid on the vast expanse of the East China Sea, the city of Wenzhou in Binhai has added beauty, known as "The County of 100 islands", "Pearl of the East China Sea" laudatory name

Yandangshan Geopark

Yandangshan Geopark is located in the western part of Pingyang County, Zhejiang province, China. It is a national AAAA-level tourist attraction, 87km north of the city.

Xiwan

The Xiwan Scenic Area is located in the southeast of Pingyang County, Wenzhou. It is characterized by numerous bays, broad shoals, secluded caves and densely covered reefs.

Nanji Islands

Nanji Island, a national nature reserve, is the main island of the Nanji Islands Marine Nature Reserve, also known as seamounts. It is located in the East China Sea, 30 nautical miles outside Aojiang estuary, Pingyang County, Zhejiang province, 50 miles from downtown Wenzhou.

Jiangxinyu

Jiangxinyu, located in the middle reaches of Ou River River north of downtown Wenzhou, is one of the "Four isolated islands of China". It lies like a natural bonsai in the middle of Ou River. The island is known as "Ou River Penglai "because of its unique scenery. Over the years, jiangxinyu has been to its unique qixiu profile on behalf of Wenzhou.
